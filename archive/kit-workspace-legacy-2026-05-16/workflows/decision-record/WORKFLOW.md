# Decision Record Workflow

Use when a technical or workflow decision should be durable.

## Process

1. Capture the decision being made.
2. List options considered.
3. Explain constraints and tradeoffs.
4. State the decision and why now.
5. Record consequences, follow-ups, and review date if needed.

## Template

```md
# Decision Record

## Context
## Options Considered
## Decision
## Rationale
## Consequences
## Follow-ups
```

Save in `_workspace/outputs/decision-records/` when useful.
