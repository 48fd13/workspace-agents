# Technical Design Review

Use before significant implementation or when reviewing a design/spec.

Check:

- problem, non-goals, and constraints are clear
- alternatives and tradeoffs are considered
- service, data, API, and deployment boundaries are explicit
- rollout, rollback, validation, and observability are covered
- security, compatibility, and failure modes are named
- design is no broader than necessary

Output: decision, key risks, required changes, and open questions.
