# Backend Service Design

Use when designing or reviewing a backend service, worker, API, or module.

Cover:

- service responsibility and non-goals
- API/endpoints or message contracts
- data model and ownership
- auth/authz and tenant boundaries
- background jobs, queues, or scheduled work
- retries, idempotency, and failure behavior
- observability: logs, metrics, traces, correlation IDs
- deployment/config/migration concerns
- validation and test strategy

Keep the design practical and scoped to the current change.
