# Mermaid Diagrams Runbook

Use Mermaid for lightweight diagrams in Markdown.

Common choices:

- `flowchart` for process or architecture flow
- `sequenceDiagram` for request/response interactions
- `classDiagram` for domain relationships
- `erDiagram` for data models

Keep diagrams small enough to maintain. Add prose when the diagram cannot explain a tradeoff.
